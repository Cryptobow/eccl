About Econfina Creek Canoe Livery LLC

We love the creek

  • We pick you up at the end of the trip and bring you back to your vehicle.
  • Shuttle service is available for people with there own vessels.
  • We have one and two person kayaks.
  • No pets allowed.
  • Cash only, no personal checks or credit cards.
  • One drivers license or picture I.D. required for each vessel rented.
